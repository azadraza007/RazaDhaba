Wazwaan made with love

Wazwan(Kashmiri: وازِوان) is a multi-course meal in Kashmiri cuisine, the preparation of which is considered an art and a point of pride in Kashmiri culture and identity. Almost all the dishes are meat-based using lamb or chicken with few vegetarian dishes. It is popular throughout the Kashmir.

our speciality

Rista

Rista is a saffron flavoured Kashmiri dish of mutton meatballs in a thin, non spicy red curry, popularly served in the legendary Kashmiri Wazwaan. (Kashmiri Meatballs in Saffron Flavoured Red Curry). Try this recipe! you will love this recipe

Kebab

They are soft, flavorsome and perfect to be served as a snack or meal with rice or bread. They are special preparations of meat infused with a host of Kashmiri condiments and grilled over skewers and are traditionally served over a Trami.

Tabak Maaz

The lip smacking recipe is made using lamb ribs, milk, ghee and a melange of spices. This recipe is served on special occasions. Even if there is no occasion you can enjoy this delightful dish lying in your bed to complete your perfect day.

Rogan Josh

This is an aromatic, mildly spicy, slightly dark. The mutton is easily cook along with yummy spices and the kasmiri chilli powder gives excellent colour to our dish. The spices added to this curry is must because that is what makes rogan josh,,,.

Goshtaba

Gushtaba is a curry made with pound mutton pieces that is mixed along with cardamom pods and ginger and cooked in a curd based curry. The cuisine uses spices like fennel seeds, ginger powder, dry red chillies and curd in most of their gravies.

Yakhni

Yakhni or Kashmiri Yakhni is a dish made with mutton or lamb with whole spices in a yogurt gravy. Whole spices make this yakhni recipe highly flavourful, ginger and fennel powder impart a delicious aroma to the curry, while the addition of yogurt makes it slightly tangy.

Tandoori Chicken

Tandoori Chicken is an iconic traditional family style smoky grilled chicken dish!. Perfect for parties or meetings. It is smoky from the grill and the smoked paprika and boasts a tapestry of earthy flavors from garam masala to ginger to garlic.

Kabargah

Kabargah is made of Ribs of young lamb/goat, cooked in milk and spices then fried in ghee. This dish uses a lot of turmeric, yoghurt and spices and does not ​make use of onions and garlic which makes this style of cooking unique.
